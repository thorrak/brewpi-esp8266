◆ numFilterSections

constexpr int CascadedFilter::numFilterSections = 3
staticconstexpr

Number of filter sections to use.

Three filter sections gives excellent filtering, without adding too much delay. For 3 sections the stop band attenuation is 3x the single section attenuation in dB. The delay is also tripled.
